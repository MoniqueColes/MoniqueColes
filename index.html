Monique Coles

Scientist

Science has been my main life's interest. I graduated from Purdue with a Bachelor Of Science in Biology and Psychology. I knew about my love of biology but discovered psychology at Purdue. I have been a scientist at Dow AgroSciences, Beckman Coulter and Roche Diagnostics. At Dow Agrosciences I worked with genetically modifying corn and rapeseed. Beckman Coulter gave me the opportunity to work with engineers testing lab automation mostly with liquid handlers. With Roche I was a development scientist working with a forensics test strip to run mitochondrial DNA for a pre-test to keep the cost of sequencing down. I took time off to raise my two children and discovered my new interest in software development through a class at Eleven Fifty Academy. I am now ready to mix my love of science with my love of computers!
