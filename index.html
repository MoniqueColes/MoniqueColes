About


Science has always been my main interest. I graduated from Purdue with a Bachelor of Science in Biology and Psychology. I knew about my love of biology but discovered psychology at Purdue. I have been a scientist at Dow AgroSciences, Beckman Coulter and Roche Diagnostics. As a scientist at Dow Agrosciences I worked with genetically modifying corn and rapeseed. Beckman Coulter gave me the opportunity to work with engineers testing lab automation mostly with liquid handlers. My position at Roche as a development scientist allowed me to work with a forensics test strip to run mitochondrial DNA for a pre-test to keep the cost of sequencing down. During my time off to raise my two children I learned all about running a business with my husband. He started an LLC and I maintained all the bookkeeping. Through learning about my husband's business I found that I also have an interest in computers. I have recently completed the full-time bootcamp in software development at Eleven Fifty Academy. I am now ready to mix my love of science with my new love of computers.
